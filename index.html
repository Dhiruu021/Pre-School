About Us

Learn more about our preschool and our approach to early childhood education

Our Story

Little Flower School was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to provide children with a foundation for lifelong learning in a nurturing, play-based environment.

Our dedicated team of early childhood educators is commited to helping each child develop socially, emotionally, physically, and intellectually.

Our Philosophy

We believe that children learn best through play and exploration. Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity, foster creatvity, and build confidence in young learners.
